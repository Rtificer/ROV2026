Solve the thruster allocation quadratic program (QP) for ROV control.

This function solves the following QP to optimally allocate forward ( \( T^+ \)) and reverse ( \( T^- \)) thruster commands:

Minimize over \( T^+, T^- \):

\[ \left\| W \left( w - \sum_{i} (T^+_i M^+_{:,i} + T^-_i M^-_{:,i}) \right) \right\|^2 + (T^+ + T^-)^\top Q (T^+ + T^-) \]

Subject to:

\[ \begin{align*} 0 &\leq T^+_i \leq 1 \\ -1 &\leq T^-_i \leq 0 \\ T_i &= T^+_i + T^-_i \in [-1, 1] \end{align*} \]

Where:

  • \( T \) ( \( n \times 1 \)): Total normalized thruster command vector, values in \( [-1, 1] \).
  • \( T_i = T^+_i + T^-_i \): Effort sent to thruster \( i \).
  • \( T^+, T^- \) ( \( n \times 1 \)): Forward and reverse thruster command vectors, constrained to [0, 1] and [-1, 0] respectively.
  • \( M^+, M^- \) ( \( 6 \times n \)): Thruster mapping matrices for forward/reverse. \( M^+_{:,i} \) describes the wrench contribution for max forward thrust, \( M^-_{:,i} \) for max reverse thrust.
  • \( W \) ( \( 6 \times 6 \)): Diagonal wrench weighting matrix.
  • \( Q \) ( \( n \times n \)): Diagonal effort weighting matrix.
  • \( w \) ( \( 6 \times 1 \)): Desired 6-DOF wrench vector (3 forces, 3 torques).

The QP is solved using OSQP. The solution is returned in \( T^+ \) and \( T^- \).
